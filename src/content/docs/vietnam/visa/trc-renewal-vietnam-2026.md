## Quick answer

If a Vietnam Temporary Residence Card (TRC) is still valid and the same legal basis continues, renewal is usually a document-readiness problem: the sponsor, passport, category, and supporting proof all need to line up before the file is accepted.

The official processing anchor is short: the TRC issuance procedure published on Vietnam's Immigration Department portal and the National Public Service Portal commonly states **5 working days after a complete dossier is received**. That does not mean a foreigner should start five working days before expiry. In practice, start preparing **30-45 days before the TRC expires**, and earlier if a work permit, passport, family document, employer record, or investor document also needs updating.

For official card fees, the current 2026 TRC card bands are:

| TRC duration | Official card fee |
|---|---:|
| Up to 2 years | 145 USD |
| More than 2 years to 5 years | 155 USD |
| More than 5 years to 10 years | 165 USD |

Do not treat every public reference to a **10 USD temporary-residence extension fee** as the TRC renewal fee. A temporary residence extension and a new TRC card issuance can be different procedures.

If the TRC has already expired, do not assume it can simply be renewed late. The case may shift into penalty handling, status resolution, and a new-card path. For broader visa context, see the main [Vietnam visa guide](/vietnam/visa/visa-guide-2026). For first-time TRC eligibility and category basics, see the [Vietnam TRC application guide](/vietnam/visa/trc-application-guide-2026).

## Is TRC renewal the same as applying for a new TRC?

Reader-facing language usually calls this “TRC renewal.” Officially, the process is closer to **applying for a new temporary residence card based on a continuing legal basis**.

That distinction matters.

If the same employer, family sponsor, investor basis, or principal applicant still supports the case, renewal may feel simpler than a first application. The reader already has a TRC, and the sponsor may already know the file history.

But it is not just paying a fee to extend the plastic card. The immigration side still needs current proof that the foreigner remains eligible. If the TRC has expired, the legal and practical handling can become less clean: the expired card may need to be considered as part of a new-card file, and penalty rules may apply if the foreigner has stayed beyond the permitted period.

Use this article as the renewal decision guide. Use the separate [TRC application guide](/vietnam/visa/trc-application-guide-2026) for first-time category eligibility, LD / DT / TT category background, and the broader TRC application process.

## Who can usually renew a Vietnam TRC?

The safest way to think about renewal is not “Do I have a TRC now?” but “Does the same legal basis still exist?”

| Current situation | Renewal decision | What must still be true |
|---|---|---|
| LD1 / LD2 work-based TRC, same employer | Conditional yes | Employer sponsor still supports the file; work permit or exemption basis remains valid; passport validity supports the requested term. |
| LD TRC, work permit expiring soon | Conditional, but check work basis first | Work permit or exemption renewal may need to happen before or alongside the TRC file. |
| LD TRC, employer changed | Not a routine renewal | New employer sponsorship and work basis need reassessment. Treat this as a new sponsor case. |
| TT TRC linked to a foreign principal applicant | Conditional yes | Principal applicant remains eligible; relationship proof and sponsor status still support the dependent file. |
| TT TRC linked to a Vietnamese spouse or family sponsor | Conditional yes | Family relationship proof and Vietnamese sponsor documents still support the case. |
| DT1 / DT2 / DT3 investor TRC | Conditional yes | Investment category and company / investment records still support the same TRC basis. |
| DT4 investor status | No normal TRC renewal path under the current Article 36 TRC list | Do not assume DT4 can be renewed into a TRC. Check category before planning. |
| Passport expires soon | Conditional / may block the requested term | A TRC must be shorter than passport validity by at least 30 days. Renew the passport first if the remaining validity is too short. |
| TRC already expired | Not a clean renewal | Penalty handling, status correction, and a new-card path may be needed. |
| E-visa or 45-day exemption user without sponsor | Not a TRC renewal | This is a first-time TRC or visa-strategy issue, not renewal. |

As of April 2026, this article treats the current TRC category framework cautiously. Vietnam has Law No. 118/2025/QH15 amending and supplementing a number of articles of 10 laws related to security and order scheduled to take effect from 1 July 2026, with changes that can affect category wording and document handling. If a renewal will be filed after that date, re-check the category and current procedure page before relying on older instructions.

## When should you start the renewal process?

### Official layer

The official procedure timing is usually expressed as **5 working days from receipt of a complete dossier**. This is the processing time after the competent authority has accepted a sufficient file.

### Practical layer

A foreigner should normally start preparing earlier, because the slow part is often not the final immigration review. The slow part is getting the file ready.

A practical planning window is:

| Situation | Safer preparation window |
|---|---:|
| Same sponsor, same category, passport valid, documents ready | 30 days before expiry |
| Work-based TRC with work permit / exemption checks | 30-45 days before expiry |
| Family-based TRC with overseas marriage / birth documents | 45 days or more if legalization / translation is still needed |
| Passport expiring soon | Renew passport first; do not wait for the TRC window |
| Employer change, sponsor change, expired TRC, or category change | Treat as a risk case, not routine renewal |

The goal is not necessarily to submit the file 45 days early. The goal is to identify blockers before the expiry date becomes urgent.

## The most important timing checks

Before preparing forms, check these five dates.

| Date to check | Why it matters |
|---|---|
| TRC expiry date | Determines whether this is still a normal renewal window or already a missed-window case. |
| Passport expiry date | The new TRC cannot run too close to passport expiry. |
| Work permit / exemption expiry date | For LD cases, the work basis can cap or block TRC renewal. |
| Sponsor document validity | Company, family, or investor records may need updating before the file is accepted. |
| Planned travel dates | Leaving Vietnam near expiry can create re-entry and filing problems if the TRC is not renewed first. |

The 30-day passport-buffer rule is grounded in Law No. 47/2014/QH13 on entry, exit, transit, and residence of foreigners in Vietnam, effective from 1 January 2015, as amended by Law No. 51/2019/QH14 and Law No. 23/2023/QH15. Article 38 of the current consolidated framework states that a TRC must be at least 30 days shorter than the remaining passport validity. This requirement applies to temporary residence card issuance regardless of category. Confirm the current implementing instruction with the sponsor or Immigration Department before relying on a tight passport-validity gap.

If travel is planned close to expiry, check the renewal plan before booking. A valid TRC can normally support exit and re-entry during its validity, but a nearly expired or expired card can create avoidable risk.

## Documents for Vietnam TRC renewal

The exact dossier depends on category and sponsor. The safest structure is to separate official core documents from practical supporting documents.

### Official core dossier

The official TRC procedure framework commonly revolves around:

- sponsor request document;
- application form for temporary residence card issuance;
- passport or international travel document;
- proof that the foreigner qualifies for the relevant TRC category.

For many cases, the practical form set includes:

| Form | Typical use |
|---|---|
| NA6 | Request form from an inviting / sponsoring organization. |
| NA7 | Request form from an individual sponsor. |
| NA8 | Temporary residence card application form for the foreign applicant. |

Form usage can depend on sponsor type and current procedure instructions. Do not reuse an old form without checking the sponsor and current immigration instructions.

### Practical documents commonly requested

In practice, sponsors, agencies, or local offices may ask for extra items. These should not be described as official nationwide requirements in every case, but they are common enough to prepare for.

| Case type | Practical documents to prepare or check |
|---|---|
| Work-based LD renewal | Work permit or work-permit exemption, employer request, enterprise registration, company seal / authorized signer information, current TRC, passport, recent temporary residence information. |
| TT dependent of foreign principal | Principal applicant’s passport and TRC / qualifying status, relationship documents, sponsor request, dependent passport, current TRC. |
| TT family case with Vietnamese sponsor | Vietnamese sponsor ID documents, household / residence information if requested, legalized or Vietnamese-issued marriage / birth / family relationship proof, applicant passport and current TRC. |
| DT1 / DT2 / DT3 investor renewal | Enterprise registration, investment registration or capital contribution records where applicable, investor-category proof, passport, current TRC. |
| Passport changed since last TRC | New passport, old passport if available, old TRC, sponsor explanation or transfer / replacement instructions from immigration. |
| Address changed | Updated temporary residence information if requested by the sponsor or immigration office. |

If a document was issued outside Vietnam, expect possible legalization, notarization, or translation questions. This is especially common for marriage certificates, birth certificates, and family-relationship documents.

## Renewal documents by TRC type

### LD1 / LD2 work-based TRC

For work-based TRC renewal, check the work basis first.

Officially, the TRC depends on the foreigner continuing to qualify under the relevant immigration category. Practically, the employer sponsor and work permit / exemption status are often the real bottleneck.

Before treating the TRC renewal as ready, confirm:

- the employer is still the sponsor;
- the job and entity still match the work-permit or exemption basis;
- the work permit or exemption remains valid long enough;
- the company can sign and stamp the sponsor request if required;
- the passport has enough validity for the requested card term.

If the employer changed, do not treat the file as a normal renewal. A new employer usually means new sponsor documents and a fresh eligibility check.

### TT family-based TRC

TT renewal depends on the sponsor relationship.

For a dependent linked to a foreign principal applicant, the principal applicant’s own status matters. If the principal applicant’s TRC, work status, investor status, or passport is expiring, the dependent file can be delayed or shortened.

For a family case linked to a Vietnamese spouse or family sponsor, the key issue is relationship proof. Vietnamese-issued records are usually simpler than overseas records. Overseas marriage or birth documents may need legalization and translation before they are accepted.

Prepare early if any family document was issued outside Vietnam.

### DT investor TRC

Investor TRC renewal depends on the investment category that still applies.

DT1, DT2, and DT3 are the main investor categories that can support TRC treatment under the current TRC list. DT4 is a high-risk boundary: do not assume a DT4 investor visa or status can be renewed into a TRC.

For investor cases, check:

- current investment category;
- enterprise / investment registration records;
- capital contribution or ownership records where relevant;
- sponsor entity documents;
- passport validity;
- whether any category changes have occurred since the last card was issued.

Investor cases can be document-heavy. If the company changed structure, ownership, registration details, or capital status, treat renewal as a fresh eligibility review.

## Vietnam TRC renewal fees in 2026

### Official card fees

Under the current 2026 fee framework, TRC card fees are based on card duration. These bands come from Circular No. 28/2026/TT-BTC on fee and charge amounts in the field of exit, entry, transit, and residence in Vietnam, effective from 1 April 2026:

| Card duration | Official fee |
|---|---:|
| Up to 2 years | 145 USD |
| More than 2 years to 5 years | 155 USD |
| More than 5 years to 10 years | 165 USD |

These are official card-issuance fee bands. The actual term granted still depends on category, passport validity, and the underlying basis. For example, a passport expiring soon can shorten the possible TRC term even if the category could otherwise support a longer card.

Fees may be handled in USD or Vietnamese dong equivalent depending on the collecting office and current fee rules. Ask the sponsor or receiving office how payment should be prepared.

### Why some pages mention 10 USD

Some public materials and service-provider pages refer to low fees such as 10 USD for temporary residence extension or related residence procedures. Do not automatically treat those as the fee for a new TRC card.

For this article, the safer rule is:

- use **145 / 155 / 165 USD** as the official TRC card fee bands;
- treat **10 USD temporary-residence extension references** as a possible different procedure unless the receiving authority confirms that specific fee applies to the reader’s case;
- separate official government fees from any agency or service fee.

### Agency or service fees

If an employer, school, family sponsor, or licensed agent helps prepare the file, their service fee is not the official government fee. It can vary by case complexity, document translation, legalization, urgency, and whether the sponsor already has clean records.

Ask for an itemized quote that separates:

- official government fee;
- translation / notarization / legalization costs;
- agency service fee;
- courier or document-handling costs;
- any fee that depends on additional immigration-office instructions.

Do not rely on a single bundled price unless it clearly states what is included.

## How long does TRC renewal take?

### Official layer

The official processing anchor is **5 working days after the complete dossier is received**.

This is useful, but it has a narrow meaning. It starts after the competent authority receives a sufficient dossier, not when the reader first asks HR, a spouse, a school, or an agent about renewal.

### Practical layer

The practical timeline can be longer because the file may need:

- work permit or exemption renewal;
- employer signature and seal;
- sponsor document update;
- family-document legalization or translation;
- passport renewal;
- temporary residence information update;
- correction of inconsistent names, dates, passport numbers, or addresses;
- response to a supplement request.

A cautious practical sequence is:

| Step | Practical timing risk |
|---|---|
| Check category and sponsor | Low if unchanged; high if employer or sponsor changed. |
| Check passport and work permit / family / investor basis | High if any document expires soon. |
| Prepare forms and sponsor documents | Medium; depends on HR, company, spouse, or family sponsor responsiveness. |
| Submit complete dossier | Medium; office may request supplement or correction. |
| Official review after acceptance | Usually anchored to 5 working days, but only after sufficient file receipt. |
| Receive card / complete handover | Depends on submission route and local instructions. |

For daily planning, assume the official review is the final stage, not the whole renewal project.

## Where is the renewal submitted?

Procedure pages may show direct submission, online submission, and postal supplement routes. As of 2026, public-service procedures may route TRC files through the National Public Service Portal (dichvucong.gov.vn), the Ministry of Public Security public-service portal, the Immigration Department portal, or the provincial immigration office, depending on sponsor and procedure type; confirm the exact receiving office before submission.

That does not mean every foreigner can complete everything alone online. TRC files are sponsor-based. The actual path may depend on:

- whether the sponsor is an organization or individual;
- whether the file is handled by the Immigration Department or provincial immigration authority;
- whether original passport or old TRC handling is required;
- whether the online portal requests later document supplements;
- whether the sponsor or agent is instructed to appear directly.

Practical rule: let the sponsor confirm the actual submission route before relying on online-only filing.

## Common Vietnam TRC renewal problems

### 1. Passport validity is too short

A TRC must be shorter than passport validity by at least 30 days. If the passport is close to expiry, the new TRC may be shortened or the file may need passport renewal first.

This is one of the easiest problems to catch early.

### 2. Work permit and TRC dates do not line up

For LD cases, the TRC renewal depends on the work basis. If the work permit or exemption expires soon, the TRC may not be granted for the expected period, or the file may not be ready.

Check the work permit or exemption before focusing on the TRC form.

### 3. Employer changed

A new employer is not just a new address on the same card. It can change the sponsor and the legal basis. Treat this as a new sponsor case.

### 4. Family documents are not ready

TT cases can be delayed by marriage certificates, birth certificates, dependent proof, or foreign-issued documents that need legalization and translation.

If the document was issued overseas, start early.

### 5. Sponsor records are outdated

Company records, seal information, signer authority, investment records, or family sponsor records may need updating. The foreigner may not notice this until the sponsor starts preparing the file.

### 6. Address or temporary residence information is inconsistent

Vietnam’s immigration and residence systems are sensitive to where a foreigner is staying. If the current address, landlord reporting, hotel reporting, or temporary residence record does not match the file, the sponsor may need to correct it before submission.

For broader housing-related registration context, see the rental safety guide’s coverage of temporary residence registration in [avoiding rental scams in Vietnam](/vietnam/housing/avoid-rental-scams-vietnam-2026).

### 7. The card already expired

Once the TRC expires, the case becomes higher risk. It may involve penalties, status correction, or a new-card path rather than a clean renewal.

Do not wait until expiry to ask whether renewal is possible.

## What happens if you miss the renewal window?

Missing the window does not produce one universal outcome. It depends on how long the card has been expired, whether the foreigner is still in Vietnam, whether the sponsor can regularize the file, and how immigration handles the case.

### Official layer

Vietnam’s newer administrative penalty framework under **Decree No. 282/2025/ND-CP on prescribing penalties for administrative violations against regulations on social security, order, and safety; prevention and combat of social issues; prevention and combat against domestic violence, effective from 15 December 2025**, includes penalties for foreigners using temporary residence papers, temporary residence extensions, or TRCs beyond the permitted period without competent authority permission.

Provincial police announcements implementing Decree 282/2025 — published on Department of Public Security portals across multiple provinces — show that fines rise by expiry duration, with published bands including:

| Expiry / overstay duration | Published fine band |
|---|---:|
| Under 16 days | 500,000-2,000,000 VND |
| 16 days to under 30 days | 5,000,000-10,000,000 VND |
| 30 days to under 60 days | 10,000,000-15,000,000 VND |
| 60 days to under 90 days | 15,000,000-20,000,000 VND |
| 90 days to under 180 days | 20,000,000-25,000,000 VND |
| 180 days to under 1 year | 25,000,000-30,000,000 VND |
| 1 year or more | 30,000,000-40,000,000 VND |

Specific portal references and the exact band applied to a given case can vary by province; confirm with the receiving immigration office before relying on a fine band as a planning anchor.

Very short expiry cases can still create administrative risk. Do not assume there is a free grace period.

### Practical layer

In practice, a missed window may require the sponsor to:

- explain the delay;
- resolve fine or penalty handling;
- prepare a new-card file;
- update residence information;
- decide whether the foreigner must exit and re-enter;
- confirm whether an e-visa or other temporary route is needed.

Do not treat “late renewal” as a normal filing choice. If the TRC has already expired, contact the sponsor, employer, family sponsor, school, or qualified immigration adviser before making travel plans.

## Can an e-visa, visa run, or 45-day exemption fix a missed TRC renewal?

These are fallback tools, not TRC renewals.

### E-visa

A Vietnam e-visa can be useful if the foreigner needs a temporary status route after leaving and re-entering, but it does not renew the TRC. If this becomes relevant, check the [Vietnam e-visa application guide](/vietnam/visa/evisa-application-guide-2026).

### Visa run

A visa run may be a contingency if the foreigner cannot maintain TRC continuity, but it carries re-entry and timing risk. It should not be the default solution for a missed TRC renewal. See the [Vietnam visa run guide](/vietnam/visa/visa-run-guide-2026) before considering this path.

### 45-day visa exemption

The 45-day visa exemption applies only to eligible passports and is not a TRC replacement. It may help some readers re-enter temporarily, but it does not solve the sponsor and TRC eligibility problem. See the [45-day visa exemption guide](/vietnam/visa/45-day-visa-exemption-vietnam-2026) for passport-specific caveats.

## Renewal checklist

Before the sponsor submits the file, confirm:

- current TRC expiry date;
- passport expiry date;
- TRC category;
- sponsor type: employer, organization, foreign principal, Vietnamese family sponsor, or investor entity;
- whether the same sponsor still applies;
- work permit or exemption validity for LD cases;
- family relationship proof for TT cases;
- investment / enterprise records for DT cases;
- temporary residence information;
- whether overseas documents need legalization or translation;
- official card fee band based on requested duration;
- whether any service fee is separate from the government fee;
- travel plans before expiry;
- fallback plan if the file is not accepted before expiry.

## Renewal vs first application: what is different?

A renewal can be easier when nothing has changed. The sponsor already knows the file, the foreigner already has a category history, and the immigration office may be familiar with the basis.

But the risk points are similar to a first application:

| Issue | First application | Renewal |
|---|---|---|
| Need eligible category | Yes | Yes, still required. |
| Need sponsor | Yes | Yes, and the sponsor must still support the file. |
| Need passport validity | Yes | Yes, and it may shorten the new card. |
| Need work / family / investor proof | Yes | Yes, updated proof may be needed. |
| Need official fee | Yes | Yes, based on card duration. |
| Risk if delayed | Application delay | Possible expiry, penalty, and status-resolution issue. |

The main renewal advantage is continuity. The main renewal danger is assuming continuity without checking the underlying documents.

## Practical decision rules

Use these rules before choosing a renewal path.

| If this is true | Do this first |
|---|---|
| Passport expires soon | Renew the passport before relying on TRC renewal. |
| Work permit expires soon | Resolve the work basis before assuming LD TRC renewal is ready. |
| Employer changed | Treat as a new sponsor case. |
| Family document was issued overseas | Check legalization / translation early. |
| TRC already expired | Get sponsor / immigration advice before travel or filing. |
| Public fee source says 10 USD | Verify whether it refers to temporary residence extension, not TRC card issuance. |
| Online submission appears available | Confirm whether original documents or sponsor appearance are still required. |
| Reader no longer has sponsor | Reassess visa strategy; this is not a normal TRC renewal. |

## FAQ

### Can a Vietnam TRC be renewed online?

Possibly for some procedure stages, because public-service pages can show online submission routes. But TRC files are sponsor-based, and practical handling may still require original documents, sponsor action, or direct office instructions. Do not assume the process is fully online from start to finish.

### Can a TRC be renewed after it expires?

It may still be possible to regularize the situation, but it should not be described as a clean renewal. An expired card can create penalty risk and may need new-card handling or other status steps.

### Is the TRC renewal fee 10 USD?

Do not assume that. For TRC card issuance in 2026, the official card fee bands are 145 / 155 / 165 USD by duration. A 10 USD reference may relate to a different temporary-residence or transfer procedure.

### How early should work-based TRC holders start?

Start at least 30-45 days before expiry, and earlier if the work permit or exemption also needs renewal. For LD cases, the work basis is often the real bottleneck.

### Does a new passport affect TRC renewal?

Yes. The new TRC term depends on passport validity, and a passport-number change can require additional handling. If the passport expires soon, renew it before relying on the TRC process.

### Is a visa run a safe backup if renewal is late?

It is a fallback, not a renewal method. Re-entry, e-visa timing, passport eligibility, and immigration discretion can all matter. Use visa-run planning only after checking safer status options.
